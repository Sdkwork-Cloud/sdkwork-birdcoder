# SDKWork Birdcoder 完整对齐计划（第三轮）

## 目标
修复审计发现的全部 43 个差距，确保三个应用根完全对齐 sdkwork-specs。

## 差距汇总

| 严重级别 | 数量 | 说明 |
|----------|------|------|
| CRITICAL | 3 | H5/Flutter sdkwork.app.config.json 缺少必需字段；PC 13个包缺少 pc 段 |
| HIGH | 14 | canonicalSpecs 缺少必需规范；scripts/tests/docs 为空；缺少包级 component.spec.json |
| MEDIUM | 20 | 缺少工具兼容 shim；bin/ 为空；config 示例不完整；tsconfig 仅覆盖 PC |
| LOW | 6 | 缺少 build:staging/test:config 脚本；security 标志全 false |

## Step 1: CRITICAL — 完善 H5 和 Flutter 的 sdkwork.app.config.json

**文件**:
- `apps/sdkwork-birdcoder-h5/sdkwork.app.config.json`
- `apps/sdkwork-birdcoder-flutter-mobile/sdkwork.app.config.json`

**操作**: 按 `APP_MANIFEST_SPEC.md` §3 补全所有必需字段：
- `backend` (profileKey, ownerMode, grantMode, platform, tenantId)
- `media` (icons, screenshots, previews, metadata)
- `publish` (status, platforms, installPlatforms, defaultPackageId, storeUrl, config)
- `environments` (development, test, staging, production — 每个含 accessUrl, deployUrl, deployEnv)
- `artifacts` (installConfig — defaultPackageId, installCommand, launchCommand, packages[])
- `release` (currentVersion, defaultChannel, latest, notes[])
- `security` (checksumRequired, signatureRequired, sbomRequired)

**参考**: 根目录 `sdkwork.app.config.json` 的完整结构。

## Step 2: CRITICAL — 解决 PC 13 个非 pc 段包

**问题**: `apps/sdkwork-birdcoder-pc/packages/` 下有 13 个包缺少 `pc` 段：
`sdkwork-birdcoder-auth`, `-commons`, `-core`, `-distribution`, `-i18n`, `-iam`, `-infrastructure`, `-settings`, `-shell`, `-skills`, `-templates`, `-types`, `-user`

**决策**: 这些是跨端共享包，已存在于根目录 `packages/`。PC app root 中的副本是冗余的。

**操作**:
1. 删除 `apps/sdkwork-birdcoder-pc/packages/` 下的 13 个非 pc 段包目录
2. 更新 `apps/sdkwork-birdcoder-pc/pnpm-workspace.yaml` 确保引用根目录共享包
3. 更新 `apps/sdkwork-birdcoder-pc/tsconfig.json` 路径别名指向根目录共享包
4. 运行 `pnpm typecheck` 验证
